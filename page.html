Interactive Computing Assignment #6

How To:

Manuever throughout the land using mouse pressed. When you click on a favela(house) a sound will play!

Concept:

My concept for this assignment was taken from my love for the Brazilian culture. I have always been fascinated with the Brazilian instruments as well as the art and style of the favelas located in the more urban/low-income areas of Brazil. There is a culture that exists that is beautiful in itself, and speaks of the deep history that lives in those areas. The music I am using is from one of my all-time favorite video games called Papo and Yo, about a little boy named Quico who is trying to cure his dad of alcoholicism. It's a beautiful story and it has influenced me a lot in my games.

My favorite part of my VR world is the music, because I believe it definitely adds to the experience. The flower is a nice touch, and brings an element of nature to my experience. I had a bit of trouble figuring out the placement of certain objects being that everything has somewhat switched now that we are using VR instead of a 2D enviornment. I think, though, that I will learn as I continue to use the A-Frame library. I have prior experience working with VR in Unity, but this was way easier and a lot more effective in allowing me to create a space in the way that I liked.

Overall, I am very pleased with my experience. I think visually it is very different, and the audio fits well with it. I definitely would like to add more to is as it has really inspired me to do more with the theme.
